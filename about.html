ABOUT US

ARCIMEDIA was established in 2004 with the objective to provide lighting design and consultancy services based on good design and engineering practice . The firm is headed by its founder and principal director Wan Abd Razak Wan Abbas who is a trained architect and is passionate about lighting.

Work with light is exciting but presents different challenges. For us every project is unique and requires specific lighting solutions. As a progressive lighting design firm, we continue to explore possibilities in creating lights and moods that are functional and visually pleasing.

Our commitment is to provide quality consultancy services through integrated and sustainable lighting solutions by our dedicated team of architect and lighting designers .
