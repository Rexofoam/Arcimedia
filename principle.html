Principal Director

Wan Abd Razak Wan Abbas

BA(Hons) Architecture BArch Manchester Metropolitan University

Manchester, United Kingdom.

Wan Abd Razak has been involved in architectural and illumination design projects since 1993. In 2004, he founded ARCIMEDIA to pursue his passion in architectural illumination . To date, he continues to lead the firm to produce creative and sustainable lighting designs .
